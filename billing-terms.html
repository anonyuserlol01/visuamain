Membership & Billing Terms

Effective July 21, 2026 · Prices are in Canadian dollars

Open House is free access, not a trial. No card is collected, there is no countdown and nobody is automatically enrolled when Open House ends.

1. Plans and prices

Current base prices are shown on the Pricing page and again in Stripe Checkout before purchase. House+ is $6.99 monthly or $59 annually. Hiring Studio is $19 monthly or $190 annually. Applicable taxes may be added and will be shown before payment. If the Pricing page and Checkout differ, do not complete payment; contact support.

2. Deliberate purchase

A paid membership begins only when the account holder selects a plan, sees the price and frequency, accepts these Billing Terms and completes Stripe Checkout. A success-page visit alone never grants paid status; VISUAREALM uses verified Stripe subscription events as the billing authority.

3. Trials

If a trial is offered, Checkout states its exact length and the date the first charge will occur. Unless you cancel before that date, the selected paid plan begins automatically and renews at its stated frequency. Trials may be limited to one per person or payment method. Open House access is not a trial and does not convert into a subscription.

4. Automatic renewal

Monthly and annual memberships renew automatically until cancelled. Stripe charges the saved payment method at the start of each billing period. We will not change an existing member’s price without advance notice and any consent required by law.

5. Cancellation

You may cancel through Profile & Settings → Membership → Manage billing, using the Stripe Customer Portal. Cancellation stops future renewal; access ordinarily continues through the paid period already purchased. If self-service is unavailable, email support@visuarealmstudios.com. We do not create unnecessary cancellation barriers.

6. Refunds and consumer rights

Except where law requires otherwise, completed billing periods are non-refundable and we do not provide partial-period credits. We will review duplicate charges, unauthorized charges, service failures and mistaken annual purchases fairly. Nothing in these terms limits cancellation, refund, cooling-off or other consumer rights that cannot be waived under applicable law.

7. Failed payments

If payment fails, Stripe may retry and notify you. Paid features may be temporarily preserved during a short recovery period, then return to Free if payment remains unresolved. Profiles, applications, safety access and account data are not deleted because payment fails.

8. Plan changes

Checkout or the Customer Portal shows the timing and amount of any upgrade or downgrade, including prorations if enabled. Review that summary before confirming. Founding Roster access is a separate, founder-issued grant and is not converted into a charge.

9. Taxes and receipts

Stripe provides transaction receipts using the billing information supplied at Checkout. You are responsible for accurate billing details. VISUAREALM will collect and remit taxes when legally required.

10. Ending the paid era

VISUAREALM may return the platform to Open House. Existing subscription records remain intact until cancelled or otherwise ended, but all members may temporarily receive equivalent launch access. We will communicate any material effect on a paid member.

11. Contact

Billing help, cancellations or refund requests: support@visuarealmstudios.com. Include the account email and Stripe receipt number; never email a full card number.
