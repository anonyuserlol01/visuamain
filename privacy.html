Privacy Policy

Effective July 21, 2026 · Version 2026-07-21 · VISUAREALM Studios ("VISUAREALM", "we", "us")

This policy explains what information Creative House collects, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and the choices you have. We collect the minimum needed to run a trustworthy creative community — and nothing we don't need.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account and eligibility details: name, email, private date of birth, guardian acknowledgement where applicable, and password (passwords are stored only as secure hashes, never in plain text).
  • Profile information: discipline, craft, city, headline, bio, experience, availability, skills, status, and links you choose to add (portfolio, IMDb, Instagram, website, reel).
  • Content you create: listings, applications, cover notes, feedback, and any documents or links you attach.
  • Membership records: plan, subscription status, trial dates, renewal dates and Stripe customer or subscription identifiers. We do not receive or store your full card number.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data needed to operate and secure the service (e.g. session and authentication tokens).
  • Limited, privacy-respecting usage information to keep the platform reliable and improve it.

2. Authentication

Accounts and sign-in are handled through our infrastructure provider using industry-standard authentication. We never see or store your raw password. Email confirmation and password-reset links are sent to the email you register.

3. Cookies and local storage

We use essential cookies and browser storage to keep you signed in and to remember convenience preferences such as saved filters and recent searches. We do not sell your data or use invasive third-party advertising trackers.

4. Analytics

We may use aggregate, de-identified analytics to understand growth and improve the product (for example, which disciplines or cities are most active). This is measured in aggregate and is not used to profile individuals for advertising.

5. Third-party services

We rely on trusted infrastructure providers for hosting, database, authentication, storage and, when paid membership opens, payment processing through Stripe. Stripe receives the payment and billing information needed to complete a purchase, manage a subscription, prevent fraud and meet its legal obligations. VISUAREALM receives the resulting customer, subscription, invoice and payment status needed to provide membership. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

6. User-generated content

Profiles, listings, and mentor information you choose to make visible are shown to other signed-in members so the community can function. Applications and their contents are visible to the member who posted the listing and to our moderation team. Consider what you share; treat other members' information with the same care you'd want for your own.

7. Moderation and safety

To keep Creative House safe we review reports, apply moderation actions (warnings, flags, suspensions, bans), and keep internal moderation records and an audit log. These records are accessible only to authorized administrators and are used strictly for safety and integrity.

8. How we use your information

  • To operate core features — profiles, listings, applications, mentorship, and messaging.
  • To secure accounts and prevent fraud, abuse, and spam.
  • To provide support and respond to your requests.
  • To improve the platform and plan future features.
  • To send essential service communications (and optional updates you can control in settings).

9. Data retention

Active account and profile information is kept while your account is open. Applications and listing records are kept while needed for the member workflow and a reasonable dispute period. Open privacy requests and safety cases are kept until resolved; limited case and audit records may remain afterward to prevent repeat abuse, establish what action was taken, or meet legal obligations. Billing and transaction records may be retained for accounting, tax, fraud and chargeback requirements. Backups expire on their normal protected rotation. Our internal retention schedule is reviewed at least annually.

10. Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings. Deletion permanently removes your profile and applications and cannot be undone. Certain content may be anonymised rather than removed where it is necessary for the integrity of others' records (for example, that a listing existed). We explain this clearly before you confirm.

11. Security

We protect data with encryption in transit, hashed credentials, and database-level access controls (row-level security) that ensure members can access only their own data and that administrative data is restricted to authorized staff. No system is perfectly secure, but we work continuously to protect your information.

12. Young members

Creative House is currently for members aged 16 and older. Date of birth is used privately to check eligibility and is not shown on profiles. A limited under-18 status may be shown to an authorized hiring member so they know guardian involvement is required; it does not reveal the date of birth. That status is calculated from the private record and clears automatically at 18. Members under 18 must confirm that a parent or guardian knows they are joining, and should involve that adult before accepting contracts, paid work, releases, travel or in-person meetings. We remove under-age accounts when identified.

13. International users

Creative House is operated from Canada and may process data in Canada and other countries where our providers operate. By using the platform you understand your information may be processed outside your home country under appropriate safeguards.

14. Your choices and rights

You can view and edit your profile, control profile visibility, withdraw optional marketing consent, request access to or correction of your data, raise a privacy complaint, and delete an eligible member account. Signed-in members can download a private ZIP of account records after session verification or start and track an assisted privacy request in Settings. Each self-service export is logged as completed. Administrator and protected founder accounts cannot be self-deleted because they are required for platform control and recovery. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights.

15. Legal compliance

We comply with applicable data-protection laws and cooperate with valid legal requests. We will resist requests that are overbroad or unlawful, and will act to protect our members' rights wherever we can.

16. Privacy incidents

We record security-safeguard breaches, assess risk, contain the issue and notify affected people and privacy regulators when required. If you believe your account or personal information was exposed, contact us immediately and include only the information needed to investigate.

17. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the platform grows. Material changes will be communicated in-app or by email, and the effective date above will be updated. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.

18. Contact

The privacy lead for VISUAREALM Studios can be reached at support@visuarealmstudios.com. Put “Privacy Request” in the subject. We will acknowledge requests, verify identity where needed and respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

See also our Terms of Service, Billing Terms and Community Standards.
