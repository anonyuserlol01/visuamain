01 / Why We Exist
Everyone asks for experience. Nobody gives experience.

Breaking into the creative industry is its own quiet impossibility. The work demands a portfolio you can only build on real sets, in real rooms, with real people: the exact rooms nobody will let you into yet.

Creative House exists to break that loop. Not by teaching from a distance, but by making things. By putting you on productions that matter, beside people who care as much as you do.

You don't learn filmmaking from a syllabus. You learn it at 2am when the shot isn't working and the room figures it out together. That's the room we're building.

Not a school. Not an agency. A home.

08 / Questions

Good To Know

Who can apply?+
Anyone passionate about storytelling. High school and university students, self-taught creatives, career changers, and emerging filmmakers, photographers, editors, actors, writers, designers and producers are all welcome.
Do I need experience?+
No. We care far more about how you show up than what's already on your résumé. Bring curiosity, reliability and a willingness to learn, and we'll build the experience together.
Is it paid?+
We're honest about this: Creative House is a community, not employment, and we won't promise a paycheck. As VISUAREALM grows, productions will continue creating paid opportunities for members. We're building toward a sustainable creative ecosystem, step by step.
How often do productions happen?+
Productions run in cycles throughout the year. Each one opens its own applications, so there's always a next chance to get involved, whether or not you were part of the last one.
Can I join if I'm still in school?+
Absolutely. Many of our creators are students. We build schedules around real productions, and being in school is no barrier to being part of the house.
Can actors apply?+
Yes. Actors are core to what we make. You'll find roles on productions and a community of collaborators to grow with between them.
Can photographers join?+
Yes. Stills, behind-the-scenes, unit photography and more. Photography is one of our departments, and there's room for your eye here.
Can I volunteer?+
Creative House is collaborative by nature. Being part of a production means contributing your craft alongside everyone else, and walking away with real credits, references and relationships.
Can I leave and return?+
Always. Once you're part of Creative House, you're always welcome back. Step away when life needs you to, and apply again when you're ready.
