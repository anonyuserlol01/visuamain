Creative House

Community Standards

The agreement every member makes to everyone else here.

Creative House exists so emerging filmmakers, cast, and crew can find each other, build real work, and be treated with respect while they do it. These standards aren't legal fine print — they're the culture. Joining means you hold yourself to them, and help hold others to them too.

Who we are

We are directors, producers, camera and lighting crews, sound people, editors, colorists, composers, actors, voice artists, photographers, and everyone in between — many at the very start of their careers. A lot of us are students, self-taught, or working without a network or a budget. That makes trust the most valuable thing on this platform. Everything below protects it.

What we expect

01
Be professional on and off set

Treat every interaction — a message, an application, a day on location — the way you'd want a producer you admire to remember you. Show up when you say you will. Communicate clearly. Credit people for their work.

02
Respect people and their craft

Disagree about the work, never demean the person. Sets are collaborative and often stressful; we hold a higher standard here, not a lower one.

03
Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination

No harassment, bullying, sexual misconduct, or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, religion, age, or background. This applies in messages, applications, and any interaction that starts on Creative House — including ones that move to set or DMs.

04
No predatory behaviour

Positions of power — casting, hiring, mentoring — are never to be used to pressure, exploit, or extract anything from another member. Quid-pro-quo of any kind is an immediate removal.

05
Protect creative work

Don't pass off others' reels, photographs, footage, scripts, or designs as your own. Portfolio theft and false credits break the one thing this community runs on. Only post work you made or can rightfully show.

06
Be who you say you are

No fake identities, inflated credits, or impersonating other creatives, studios, or productions. Represent your experience honestly — "emerging" is respected here.

07
No scams, fraud, or exploitation

No fake productions, pay-to-audition schemes, hidden fees, or "opportunities" that exist to collect money, data, or free labour under false pretenses. Unpaid and deferred roles are allowed — but they must be labelled honestly.

08
Don't ghost repeatedly

Plans change and that's fine — but accepting roles and vanishing, or casting people and disappearing, wastes real time and money for creatives who have little of either. Repeated ghosting is a moderation matter.

09
No spam

Don't mass-message, repost the same listing endlessly, or use Creative House to funnel people to unrelated services. Keep the space signal, not noise.

10
Keep it lawful

Nothing illegal — including unsafe set practices, working conditions that violate local law, or content that exploits or endangers anyone, especially minors.

What happens when standards are broken

We moderate with judgement, not just rules. Depending on severity and history, a member may receive:

  • A warning — a private note from the moderation team, acknowledged and cleared.
  • A flag — the account is placed under active review while we look into a concern; some actions may be limited.
  • A suspension — access is paused. Suspensions can be appealed.
  • A ban — for serious or repeated breaches, the account is permanently closed.

Harassment, predatory behaviour, fraud, and content endangering minors can result in immediate removal without prior warning.

Appeals

If you believe a moderation decision was made in error, you can ask us to review it. We read every appeal and respond. Email support@visuarealmstudios.com with your account email and what you'd like us to reconsider.

Reporting

If someone makes you feel unsafe, tries to exploit you, or breaks these standards, tell us. Use the feedback button inside the platform or email support directly. Reports are read by our team and never shown to the person you're reporting.

By creating an account you agree to these Community Standards and to helping keep Creative House a place worth being part of. Thank you for building it with us.
